SECTION 1 PROJECT OVERVIEW & OBJECTIVES
1. Project Overview & Objectives
Vouchiqo is India's first verified savings platform with a built-in Expired Offer Revival mechanism. The
platform serves two distinct user groups simultaneously: end customers who want genuine, working verified
offers, and merchant-businesses who want a performance-only channel to acquire customers through
verified promotional offers.
Unlike existing discount platforms (CouponDunia, GrabOn), Vouchiqo's core differentiator is verification at
the source — every offer is tested before going live — and the Expired Offer Revival feature, which allows
customers to recover value from expired codes by requesting merchant-approved regeneration. No other
platform in India currently offers this.
1.1 Platform Architecture Overview
The platform has three distinct sides that must operate independently but share a single underlying
database and API:
• Customer-Facing Platform — the public website where end users browse, search, save, and redeem
verified offers
• Merchant Portal — a separate authenticated dashboard where registered businesses manage their
listings, subscriptions, campaigns, analytics, and offer revival requests
• Admin Panel — an internal operations panel for the platform owner to manage merchant approvals, offer
verification, subscription billing oversight, and platform-wide content
