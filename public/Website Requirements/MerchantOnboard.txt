MODULE 1: THE B2B ONBOARDING ENGINE (THE 
MULTI-STEP REGISTRATION WIZARD) 
When a merchant accesses the Vouchiqo web portal and selects "Place your 
business and offers with Vouchiqo", they must be funneled through a mobile-first, 4
step data collection wizard. All fields must run real-time frontend validation prior to 
allowing step progression.

MODULE 2: INDIAN GST-COMPLIANT INVOICING & 
FINANCIAL ENGINE 
This will act as an acceptance matrix for all the merchant types to accept it as a 
declaration. All invoicing generated within the admin ecosystem for Cost-Per-Acquisition 
(CPA) commission metrics, campaign, or paid plans must align with Indian tax laws 
under the CGST, SGST, and IGST frameworks. 
3.1 Statutory Classification Matrix (Services Provided by Vouchiqo) 
The performance invoice generated by your platform must list services under specific 
tax categories depending on how you structure individual line items: 
1. Online Information Services / Digital Platform Services: (For monthly 
access/platform subscription plans). 
o Recommended SAC Code: 998315 (Hosting and IT infrastructure 
provisioning services). 
2. Advertising Services / Marketing Services / Lead Generation / CPA / CPL: 
(For variable success-based billing generated when a consumer checks out at a 
physical store using a Vouchiqo coupon). 
o Recommended SAC Code: 998365 (Advertising space or time allocation, 
or direct lead generation support). 
3.2 Statutory Tax Calculations & Place of Supply (POS) Logic 
The system must automatically calculate tax allocations based on the geographical 
location of the partner merchant: 
• Vouchiqo Legal Operating Base: Jharkhand (State Code: 20). 
• The Rule of Intrastate Commerce: If the merchant’s onboarded billing address 
is inside Jharkhand (Ranchi, Jamshedpur, Dhanbad, Bokaro), the billing layout 
calculates a strict 18% split: 
o 9% CGST (Central Goods and Services Tax) 
o 9% SGST (State Goods and Services Tax) 
• The Rule of Interstate Commerce (Future Provisioning): If a merchant 
registers an account using an out-of-state billing registration (e.g., West Bengal 
or Bihar), the platform calculates an un-split: 
o 18% IGST (Integrated Goods and Services Tax).
