1.17  FAQ Section 
Element 
Content / Specification 
Current FAQs (3 exist — expand to 6) 
Keep the 3 existing FAQ headers. Add 3 more 
below. 
Q1 (existing) 
A1 
How does Vouchiqo verify offer codes? 
Our team tests every offer before it goes live on 
Vouchiqo. We simulate a real purchase, confirm 
the discount applies exactly as described, and 
only then publish it with a Vouchiqo Verified 
badge and a verification date. If you find a non
working offer, tap 'Report' — we re-test within 4 
hours. 
Q2 (existing) 
What is the Expired Offer Revival System? 
A2 
When you find an offer that has already expired, 
don't give up on it. Submit it on Vouchiqo through 
our Revival page. We contact the merchant and 
ask them to reactivate it specifically for you. If 
they approve, you receive a fresh working offer 
by email within 48 hours. This service is 
completely free. 
Q3 (existing) Is Vouchiqo free to use for customers? 
A3 Yes, completely free. Creating an account, finding 
offers, copying codes, submitting Revival 
requests, and tracking your savings are all free. 
Vouchiqo earns from merchant partners, not 
from you. 
Q4 (ADD) How is Vouchiqo different from other deal sites? 
A4 Every other platform lists codes submitted by 
merchants or users without testing them — 
broken offers are common. Vouchiqo tests every 
offer first. We also offer the only Expired Offer 
Revival feature in India — and a Nearby Offers 
map showing verified deals from businesses 
physically close to you. 
Q5 (ADD) What is the Nearby Offers feature? 
A5 Nearby Offers shows you verified deals from 
businesses near your current location. Set your 
city and locality, and Vouchiqo shows you a map 
of local businesses with active verified offers — 
so you can plan your visit knowing the offer is real 
before you leave home. 
Q6 (ADD) How do I know if a code will still work tomorrow? 
A6 Each offer shows the date it was last verified by 
our team. We re-test all active offers every 30 
days and immediately on any user report. If an 
offer expires or breaks between verification 
cycles, it is pulled from the site within 4 hours of 
being reported.

expired codes at checkout. You find a deal, add

discount is gone. The trust is broken. We built 
Vouchiqo to fix that — not with a different 
aggregator, but with a different standard. Every 
offer on Vouchiqo is tested by our team before it 
reaches your screen. That's the commitment we 
make, and we make it every time.

the tile showroom, the restaurant, the salon — 
don't have access to the digital marketing tools 
that national brands do. Vouchiqo gives them 
one: a verified, performance-only channel where 
they pay only when it works. We're building from 
here, and expanding from here. 
Verification commitment section heading The Verified Standard

Vouchiqo, it means something specific: our team 
manually tested this offer, confirmed the 
discount applies, and checked the terms match 
exactly what's published. The date you see on 
every offer is the date we last confirmed it. If it 
breaks, it's off the site within 4 hours of a report.
